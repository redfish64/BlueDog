BlueDog Tracker

A Wear OS companion that spots nearby Bluetooth trackers so you always know whether your pup is still within range – even when you leave your phone at home.

Why BlueDog?

Traditional Bluetooth trackers regularly rotate their addresses. In a crowded city that makes long-term surveillance tricky – but out on forest trails or quiet beaches the only devices you should see are the ones you packed. BlueDog leans into that reality and gives you a fast, wrist-first view of the trackers attached to your dog’s collar.

  • Phone-free walks: start a scan directly on your watch and glance at the dial to confirm your dog’s tracker is still nearby.
  • Instant awareness: Shows last time BT devices were detected and the signal strength encoded as easy to decipher color coded slices.
  • Custom alerts: turn on the global chime and vibration so you can tell when your dog is nearby without looking at the screen.

How It Works

  1. Attach a Bluetooth tag (AirTag, Tile, Chipolo, etc.) to your dog’s collar.
  2. Launch BlueDog Tracker on your Wear OS watch and tap Start Scan.
  3. As devices are discovered they appear on the circular dial. The colour intensity mirrors signal strength.
  4. Tap a slice to open the device overlay and block noisy strangers if the dial feels crowded.

Interface Highlights

  • Live Scanner Dial: only active devices are shown; closer trackers glow brighter.
  • Device Overlay: Click on a BT address slice to see signal strength, last seen time, and a quick action to block distractions.
  • Global Alerts: enable chime and/or vibration so every new device discovery gets your attention.
  • Block List: hide persistent non-dog devices (smart watches, earbuds) from the dial.

Quick Start Checklist

  • Ensure Bluetooth is enabled on the watch and grant scan/connect/location permissions.
  • Use the Settings screen to tweak vibration duration, chime, and other settings.
  • Block any background devices (earbuds, fitness trackers) so the dial stays focused on your pup.

Tips & Limitations

  • Bluetooth address rotation means BlueDog shines in low-noise environments. In busy downtown areas the results will churn constantly.
  • AirTags hide their identifiers, but they still broadcast presence – BlueDog shows the active tag even though it does not pair to the AirTag.
  • The dial always reflects what’s around you right now, keeping the interface simple and free of stale data.
