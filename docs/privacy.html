Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Learn how BlueDog Tracker handles your data.

Effective Date: November 7, 2025

1. Overview

Blue Dog ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information or data from users of Blue Dog.

Our app does not:

  • Collect or transmit personal or sensitive user data.
  • Access or store user information on external servers.
  • Use analytics, advertising, or tracking tools.

3. Permissions and Device Access

Blue Dog requires certain device permissions to function properly:

  • Bluetooth access: Used to scan for and connect to nearby Bluetooth devices.
  • Location permission: Required by the Android operating system to enable Bluetooth device scanning. Blue Dog does not use, store, or share your location data in any way.

All data detected via Bluetooth remains on your device and is used only for the app's core functionality.

4. Children's Privacy

Because we do not collect or share any data, Blue Dog is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13.

5. Third-Party Services

Blue Dog does not use or integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

6. Changes to This Policy

If our data practices ever change, we will update this Privacy Policy and notify users through the app or our Google Play listing before any changes take effect.
